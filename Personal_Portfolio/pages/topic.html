My Hobbies

SKATING

Skating is a thrilling hobby that I enjoy during my free time. Whether it's skating or Roll Bal Game,oll ball is a team sport that combines elements of basketball and roller skating.Players on roller skates try to score goals by throwing a ball into the opponent's net while defending their own. Both roller skating and roll ball provide an exciting way to stay active and socialize while embracing the joy of gliding on wheels. Skating is more than just a sport for me. it's a profound passion that brings me an indescribable sense of joy and freedom. Every time I step onto my skates, I'm reminded of the simple yet profound pleasure of doing what I love.Because of my passion I became a National Player of Roll Ball and Road Racing.

TRAVELLING

Traveling is not just a hobby; it's a deep passion of mine. I love exploring new places, especially when I can enjoy the peace of nature and the company of friends. My big dream is to visit all twelve Jyotirlingas, which are important shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva. This goal allows me to connect with the rich culture and spirituality of my country. Besides this, I also want to visit famous and iconic places to learn about their history and culture. Traveling is more than a pastime; it's my way of experiencing life, creating lasting memories, and finding joy in the beauty of the world.Traveling with friends makes the journey even more special as we create cherished memories together. I believe that every place has a story to tell, and I'm on a quest to explore those tales while finding inner peace in the lap of nature.

MOVIES

Movies are not just a pastime for me; they are a source of sheer enjoyment and a window to a world of diverse emotions and genres. I find myself particularly drawn to the magic of romantic comedies, the adrenaline rush of action-packed films, and the suspense of thrilling stories. Among my all-time favorite movies, "Pirates of the Caribbean" and "Resident Evil" stand out as unforgettable adventures, while "Sita Raman" captivated me with its powerful storytelling. Additionally, when it comes to the world of web series, "Stranger Things" has held me in its gripping, supernatural narrative. These cinematic experiences are not just hobbies but a cherished part of my life, offering an escape into captivating worlds and stories that leave a lasting impact.

Video Gallery

Rollball is a captivating and dynamic sport that uniquely blends roller skating with elements of handball and basketball. In this fast-paced game, two teams of six players each compete to score goals by skillfully maneuvering on roller skates and throwing a ball into the opponent's net. The game demands a combination of skating agility, teamwork, and strategy. Players need to exhibit exceptional balance and coordination while engaging in offensive and defensive plays. Rollball is a thrilling spectacle that not only showcases the athleticism and skill of its participants but also provides an exciting experience for spectators. To fully grasp the concept and intricacies of this sport, watching a video demonstration can be incredibly enlightening and enjoyable.
